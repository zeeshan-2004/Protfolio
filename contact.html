FAQ

Common questions answered

Quick answers to help you decide whether we're a good fit.

01 What type of projects do you work on?

Custom WordPress design and development, PHP customization, and modern frontend interfaces. I specialize in building tailored themes from scratch and developing plugins to extend functionality exactly how you need it.

02 How long does a typical website project take?

A standard 5-page custom WordPress website typically takes 3 to 4 weeks from discovery to launch. More complex applications or e-commerce builds generally require 6 to 8 weeks depending on the scope and requirements.

03 Do you offer website maintenance after delivery?

Yes. Every project includes 30 days of complimentary post-launch support. Beyond that, I offer ongoing monthly retainer packages that cover updates, security monitoring, backups, and regular performance optimization.

04 What is your payment structure?

For most projects, I require a 50% deposit upfront to secure your spot in my schedule. The remaining 50% is tied to project milestones or final delivery. Larger projects can be broken down into three or four milestone payments.

05 Can you work with my existing WordPress site?

Yes, I frequently take over existing WordPress sites for performance optimization, plugin development, or major refactoring. I will conduct a full technical audit first to ensure the existing codebase is viable to work on.

06 Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. I work with clients globally across all time zones. We utilize async communication tools like Slack, Notion, and email, along with scheduled check-in calls to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of geography.
